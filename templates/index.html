Welcome to Lung & Colon Cancer Prediction Service

Colon Cancer:

Colon cancer affects the large intestine (colon) and is one of the leading cancer types worldwide. It develops when abnormal cells in the colon grow uncontrollably, forming polyps that can become cancerous over time. Early detection through screenings is vital for effective treatment.

2- Lung Cancer:

Lung cancer is a challenging condition that starts in the lungs and can spread rapidly. It is often associated with smoking, but non-smokers can also be affected. Early diagnosis is crucial for optimal outcomes, and screenings play a key role in identifying potential issues.

There are some tips for preventing Colon and Lung Cancer:

Colon Cancer:

*Regular Screenings: Schedule routine colonoscopies for early detection of polyps or abnormalities.
*Healthy Diet: Adopt a diet rich in fiber, fruits, and vegetables, and limit red meat consumption.
*Active Lifestyle: Regular exercise contributes to overall colon health.

Lung Cancer:

*Quit Smoking: If you smoke, quitting is the most effective preventive measure.
*Avoid Secondhand Smoke: Limit exposure to secondhand smoke to reduce risk.
*Protective Gear: If working in environments with carcinogenic substances, use proper protective gear.
